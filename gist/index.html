How it works

Honest about what it is

Gist is a news aggregator. It pulls articles from established outlets, compresses each one into a neutral ~60-word summary, and shows you the source name and a link to the full article on every card.

  • 📰
    Source on every card The outlet name and a "Read full story" link are always visible — we never obscure where the news came from.
  • ✍️
    Neutral paraphrase, not copy-paste Each summary is rewritten to inform, not to provoke. No word-for-word reproduction of the original.
  • 🔄
    Fresh content The feed is updated continuously. Articles older than 72 hours are automatically removed from the deck.
  • 🔒
    No account required No sign-up, no tracking. Bookmarks and preferences are stored only on your device.
