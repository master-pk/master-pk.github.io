Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

Gist does not require an account, does not collect your personal information, and does not sell your data to anyone.

Who we are

Gist ("the app") is developed and maintained by Prakash Mishra. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at everease.sup@gmail.com.

What we collect

We collect as little as possible. Here is a full account:

Information stored on your device only

The following data is saved locally on your device and is never transmitted to us:

  • Bookmarked articles
  • Sections you have enabled or disabled
  • Theme preference (light / dark / system)
  • Daily reminder time and on/off state
  • Which cards you have already seen

This data is not backed up to any server. Uninstalling the app removes it permanently.

Information we receive from your use of the app

The Gist app fetches news cards from our backend (hosted on Supabase). These requests include your device's IP address as part of normal internet traffic. We do not log or store IP addresses.

Advertising — Google AdMob

Gist displays advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data to show you relevant ads, including:

  • Advertising ID (Android Ad ID)
  • Device type and operating system version
  • Approximate location (country/region level)
  • App interaction data

This data is collected and processed by Google under their own privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy.

You can opt out of personalised ads at any time in your device settings under Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID.

Crash reporting

The app is built with Expo (by EAS). Expo may collect anonymised crash and error reports to help us identify and fix bugs. These reports do not contain personally identifiable information.

Children's privacy

Gist is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
